Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables
Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or vegetable oil (for cooking)
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • Cooked rice, for serving
  • Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish
Instructions:
  1. Marinate the chicken: In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and rice vinegar. Toss the chicken slices in the marinade and let it sit for 10-15 minutes.
  2. Stir-fry the chicken: Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry for 5-6 minutes, until browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. Stir-fry the vegetables: In the same skillet, add the remaining oil. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, and saut√© for about 1 minute. Add the bell pepper, carrot, zucchini, and snap peas. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Combine chicken and vegetables: Return the chicken to the skillet and stir to combine. Drizzle sesame oil over the mixture and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve: Serve the stir-fry over rice, garnished with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
