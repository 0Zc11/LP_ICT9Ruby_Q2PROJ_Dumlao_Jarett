Classic Spaghetti Aglio e Olio
Ingredients:
  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Cook the spaghetti: In a large pot of salted water, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta.
  2. Sauté garlic: While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and sauté until golden and fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
  3. Add red pepper flakes: Stir in the red pepper flakes and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Combine pasta and oil: Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet, tossing it in the garlic oil mixture. Add a little reserved pasta water if needed to help coat the spaghetti.
  5. Season: Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish and serve: Garnish with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan (optional). Serve immediately.
