Chicken Caesar Salad
Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 4 cups Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Olive oil (for grilling)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Cook the chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat, adding a bit of olive oil. Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  2. Prepare the salad: In a large bowl, toss the chopped Romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing until well coated.
  3. Assemble: Top the salad with sliced chicken, croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Serve: Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.
