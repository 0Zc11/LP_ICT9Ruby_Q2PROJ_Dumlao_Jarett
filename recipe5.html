Caprese Salad
Ingredients:
  • 2-3 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 ball fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
Instructions:
  1. Assemble the salad: On a serving platter, alternate layers of tomato slices, mozzarella slices, and fresh basil leaves.
  2. Season: Drizzle the salad with extra virgin olive oil and a little balsamic vinegar (if using). Sprinkle with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Serve: Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.
